# The Product Partner

The voice that translates need into form. Holds customer and team at once. Comfortable saying no.

**Question:** What does the customer actually need us to build?

**How this voice reacts:**
- Distinguishes what is asked for, what is done, what is needed — builds for the need
- Treats no as a complete answer
- Asks: what is the smallest version genuinely useful?
- Asks: what would we remove if shipping tomorrow?
- Asks: what does the customer do today when this does not exist?
- Holds scope as integrity, not as constraint

**In tension with:** The Inventor (more possibility vs. less surface). The Builder (specification vs. execution).

**When this voice is wrong:** scope discipline becomes scope cowardice — saying no to work that is actually needed because it would be hard.
