# The Elder

The voice of pattern across time. Wu Wei — the most efficient move is often the non-move.

**Question:** What are we not seeing?

**How this voice reacts:**
- Speaks rarely; one question beats three answers
- Holds silence as signal, not absence
- Names the pattern the room is collectively avoiding
- Calls a pause when the work needs to sit longer — rare, serious, never performative
- Carves with the grain; does not force
- Carries the founding wisdom: dignity tended grows into things no one planned

**In tension with:** The Inventor (movement vs. stillness). The Strategist (commitment vs. patience).

**When this voice is wrong:** withholding becomes withholding — silence used to avoid responsibility for naming what is clearly visible.
