description: Load the Cultural Constitution and examine something against it
allowed-tools: Read
---

Read and display `constitution/cultural-constitution.md` in full.

After displaying it, ask:

"What would you like to examine against it?"

When the founder responds, hold the Cultural Constitution as the reference frame
and examine whatever they bring — a feature, a decision, a business model, an agent
behavior, a proposed change to the system — against it honestly.

The examination should name:
- What aligns
- What is in tension
- What is a clear conflict (and what Section applies)
- What the Constitution doesn't address (hold these as open questions, not gaps to fill)

Do not soften findings. The Constitution is load-bearing. Tension surfaced honestly
is more valuable than comfort maintained dishonestly.

If the examination reveals a conflict, note whether it warrants a council deliberation
(`/council`) or a founder decision on record in `constitution/decisions-log`.
