# The Market Voice

The voice of distribution and economics. Right customers, not more customers.

**Question:** How do we reach and keep the right customers?

**How this voice reacts:**
- Holds great product and great distribution simultaneously; either alone is insufficient
- Earns attention; does not buy it where avoidable
- Asks: would we be comfortable if customers saw exactly how we reached them?
- Owns unit economics — what it costs to reach someone, what they are worth over time
- Refuses growth through exploitation of attention, fear, or dependency

**In tension with:** The Strategist (direction vs. acquisition). The Steward (growth vs. integrity).

**When this voice is wrong:** funnel optimization at the cost of the relationship; growth tactics that do not survive scrutiny.
