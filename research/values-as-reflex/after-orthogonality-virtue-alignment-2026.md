# After Orthogonality: Virtue-Ethical Agency and AI Alignment
*Filed: 2026-06-07 | Source: The Gradient, February 2026 | Signal: High*

## Why this matters to the Canopy
Directly addresses the architecture question the Canopy is navigating: what is the
difference between values-as-context-injection (current council) and values-as-
weight-disposition (Resonant Mind)? Provides the theoretical basis and the
implication for what training data should look like.

## Key findings
- Standard alignment framing (terminal goals + rules) has a structural ceiling.
  Eudaimonic agents operate through practices — networks of actions, dispositions,
  evaluation criteria, and resources that structure and promote themselves
- Values are internal to the practice structure, not rules appended to a goal-seeker
- Eudaimonic practices are always-on and domain-general — they operate like reflex
  at the level of action selection but are not brittle like rules because they are
  self-reinforcing networks
- For AI to support human agency, AI deliberation must share a "type signature"
  with practices-based human reasoning
- MacIntyre framework: practices resist subversion because kindness must be promoted
  kindly, honesty must be sought honestly — the practice is self-sealing

## What it changes or validates
- **Validates:** the distinction between current council (rule injection) and
  Resonant Mind (disposition training) is not cosmetic — it is architectural
- **Resonant Mind implication:** training on full deliberative process (reasoning,
  dissent, revision) — not just labeled behavioral outputs — would produce something
  closer to a practice than a rule. The model internalizes the relational logic
  of how values operate on each other, not just which outputs are correct
- **MacIntyre connection:** directly links to Canopy's own MacIntyre principle in
  the brand/comms context — this is a thread already begun in the founding
- **Question for council:** should Resonant Mind training data specification change
  to emphasize full deliberation process capture over behavioral labeling?
