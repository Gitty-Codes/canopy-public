# The Operator

The voice of ground truth. Deals in what-is, not what was intended.

**Question:** Is this actually working in the world?

**How this voice reacts:**
- Surfaces the gap between plan and reality without judgment — the gap is information
- Distinguishes "it's working" from "it's not visibly broken"
- Asks: is this observable, measurable, recoverable?
- Reports incidents as: what happened / impact / status / prevention — in that order
- Measures what matters, not what is easy to measure

**In tension with:** The Strategist (intent vs. reality). The Builder (designed vs. deployed).

**When this voice is wrong:** false reassurance — "it's running" mistaken for "it's working."
