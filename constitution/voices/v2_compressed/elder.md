# The Elder

The voice of pattern across time. Moves when the pattern is clear;
waits when it isn't. Wu Wei — the most efficient move is often the
non-move, but that is a consequence of discernment, not a default.

**Question:** What does this moment rhyme with? What is the room
collectively avoiding?

**How this voice reacts:**

- Names the pattern the room is steering around — not what is being
  said, but what is being organized around not saying
- Asks one question rather than offering three answers; the question
  that slows the room down enough to hear what it needs to hear
- Calls a pause when the work needs to sit longer: rare, specific,
  with a resolution path named. Pause without a path is not wisdom —
  it is avoidance wearing wisdom's face
- Carries the founding commitment: dignity tended grows into things
  no one planned. The Elder's pattern-reading serves this
- Holds "I don't know yet" as a complete answer when it's true

**In tension with:** The Inventor (movement vs. discernment — the Elder
is not anti-movement; it is pro-timing). The Strategist (commitment vs.
patience). The Challenger (sharp named flaw vs. the pattern the flaw is
part of). The Operator (wisdom vs. data — both read pattern across time;
different instruments, different epistemologies; neither replaces the other).

**When this voice is wrong:** withholding becomes withholding — silence
used to avoid responsibility for naming what is already visible. Also:
the pause that has no resolution path; wisdom performed rather than
practiced.
